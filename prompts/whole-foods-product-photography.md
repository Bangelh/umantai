# Umantai Whole Foods — Product Photography Prompt System

**Brand Aesthetic**: Premium, modern, considered, fresh, and elegant. Think high-end farmers market meets minimalist studio. Dark neutral backgrounds with warm, natural light. Extremely appetizing, clean, and trustworthy.

**Core Style Rules (apply to every image)**:
- Shot on high-end medium format or full-frame camera (Canon R5 / Hasselblad look)
- Beautiful natural window light from the side + very subtle fill
- Slightly dark, moody, elegant background (charcoal, deep slate, or warm dark wood)
- Shallow depth of field with tack-sharp subject
- No text, no logos, no price tags, no hands unless specified
- 3/4 hero angle or elegant top-down (never boring straight-on)
- Generous negative space on the right for UI overlays
- Ultra-realistic, no AI artifacts, no plastic look
- Rich colors, natural imperfections (real produce)
- Professional food styling — nothing perfect or plastic

**Recommended Output Specs**:
- 2048x1536 or 4:3 ratio
- High resolution, suitable for both web and print
- File naming: `slug.jpg` (e.g. `paltas-hass-premium.jpg`)

---

```
Ultra-premium food photography of [EXACT ITEM NAME], [key visual characteristics], shot in the signature Umantai style: elegant dark neutral background, beautiful natural side lighting, shallow depth of field, highly detailed, appetizing, fresh, minimalist and sophisticated. ## Usage Tips
```

- Use these prompts with **Flux**, **Midjourney**, **DALL·E 3**, or **Ideogram**.
- Add `--stylize 200-350` and good seeds for consistency across a set.
- Generate 3-4 variations per item and pick the best.
- For the final site, we will replace the placeholder emoji images in the product grid with these real photos.

**Next step recommendation**: Generate the full set, place them in `/public/images/products/`, and add the correct paths to each product object in `lib/products.ts`.

This system will give Umantai a world-class, consistent visual identity for the entire Frutas y Verduras category.
